Welcome to Rehabflex

Where Recovery Meets Results
At Rehabflex Advanced Physiotherapy and Sports Clinic, we’re committed to helping you get back to doing what you love – pain-free, stronger, and faster. Founded by Omkar Dole, a passionate physiotherapist dedicated to evidence-based care and advanced rehab techniques, our clinic combines hands-on therapy with modern tools to deliver real, lasting results. Whether you’re an athlete, a desk job warrior, or recovering from surgery, your healing journey starts here.
